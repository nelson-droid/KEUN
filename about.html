About Our Organization

Pioneering Environmental Conservation in Kakamega Since 2020

Our Mission

To promote environmental conservation and sustainable development in Kakamega through community engagement, innovative solutions, and collaborative partnerships.

Our Vision

A thriving, sustainable Kakamega where nature and communities flourish together, setting a benchmark for environmental stewardship in Kenya.

Our Journey

Founded in 2023, Kakamega Environment Uplift Network (KEUN) emerged from a grassroots movement of passionate environmentalists and community leaders. What began as local tree-planting initiatives has grown into a comprehensive environmental conservation organization.

Our journey is marked by successful partnerships with local communities, government agencies, and international organizations. Through these collaborations, we've implemented sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and local livelihoods.
