Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved?

You can join our initiatives by becoming a member, volunteering for our projects, or supporting our cause through donations. Contact us to learn more about current opportunities.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily operate in Kakamega County, focusing on environmental conservation, sustainable agriculture, and community development projects throughout the region.

Can organizations partner with you?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision for environmental conservation. Contact us to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

Do you offer training programs?

We conduct various training programs on sustainable farming, environmental conservation, and community development. Check our projects page or contact us for upcoming sessions.
